## Solar Installation Process — 6 Steps

### Step 1: Free Consultation & Site Assessment
- Free on-site or virtual consultation
- Evaluate your roof, electrical panel, shading, and energy usage
- Review your utility bills to design the optimal system
- Timeline: Same week as inquiry

### Step 2: Custom System Design
- Our engineers design a system tailored to your home and energy needs
- Equipment selection (panels, inverters, batteries)
- Production modeling and savings estimate
- You review and approve the design
- Timeline: 1-2 weeks

### Step 3: Permits & Procurement
- We handle ALL permits (city, utility, HOA)
- Order and receive all equipment
- No work required from you
- Timeline: 2-4 weeks (varies by city)

### Step 4: Professional Installation
- Our certified in-house crew installs your system
- Typical residential install: 1-3 days
- Clean, professional work with minimal disruption
- Timeline: 1-3 days on site

### Step 5: Inspection & Grid Connection
- City building inspection
- Utility interconnection approval (Permission to Operate / PTO)
- System activated and connected to grid
- Timeline: 2-6 weeks (utility dependent)

### Step 6: Monitoring & Maintenance
- System monitoring setup (Tesla app or Enphase app)
- 25-year panel warranty, 10-15 year inverter/battery warranty
- Ongoing support from Nason Solar

## Total Timeline
- From contract to PTO: typically 8-16 weeks
- Most of the wait time is permits and utility approval, not installation itself
