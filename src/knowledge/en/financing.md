## Solar Financing Options

### Solar Loans
- $0 down payment options available
- 0% introductory APR options available (varies by lender)
- Loan terms: 10, 15, 20, or 25 years
- You OWN the system from day one — eligible for all incentives
- Monthly payments often lower than current electric bill
- Fixed interest rates available

### Solar Lease
- $0 down, fixed monthly payment
- The leasing company owns the system
- Lower savings compared to ownership, but zero upfront cost
- Typical lease term: 20-25 years
- Maintenance included by leasing company

### Power Purchase Agreement (PPA)
- $0 down
- Pay only for the electricity the system produces (per kWh rate)
- Rate is typically lower than utility rate
- The PPA provider owns and maintains the system
- Good option for those who want simplicity

### PACE Financing
- Property Assessed Clean Energy
- Financed through your property tax bill
- No credit score requirement
- Transfers with property if you sell
- Longer terms available (up to 25 years)

### Cash Purchase
- Best overall ROI — no interest costs
- Fastest payback period
- Full ownership of all incentives and credits
- Recommended for those who can afford the upfront investment
