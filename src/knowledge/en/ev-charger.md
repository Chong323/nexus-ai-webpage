## EV Charger Installation Services

### Level 2 Home Charging
- 240V dedicated circuit installation
- Charges most EVs fully overnight (8-10 hours)
- Much faster than standard 120V outlet (Level 1)
- Price: ~$1,500 - $3,000 installed (includes charger, wiring, permit)

### Compatible with All EV Brands
- Tesla (Wall Connector)
- Ford, Chevrolet, Hyundai, BMW, Mercedes, Rivian, etc.
- Universal J1772 and NACS connectors

### Solar + EV Charger Combo
- Charge your EV with free solar energy
- Maximize savings by charging during solar production hours
- Smart chargers can schedule charging for optimal solar usage
- Adding an EV charger to a solar installation is cost-effective (shared electrical work)

### What's Included
- Site assessment and electrical panel evaluation
- Charger unit supply and installation
- Electrical wiring and dedicated circuit
- Permitting and inspection
- Panel upgrade if needed (additional cost may apply)
