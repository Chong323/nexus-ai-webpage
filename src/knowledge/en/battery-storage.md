## Why Battery Storage?
- Store excess solar energy for use during evening peak hours (saves money under NEM 3.0)
- Backup power during grid outages and PSPS (Public Safety Power Shutoff) events
- Reduce dependence on the grid
- Maximize self-consumption of solar energy
- Critical for optimal savings under California's NEM 3.0 policy

## Tesla Powerwall 3

- Integrated solar inverter
- Whole-home backup capable

## How Many Batteries Do I Need?
- 1 battery: Partial home backup, peak-shaving for savings
- 2 batteries: Whole-home backup for most homes
- 3+ batteries: Large homes or full off-grid capability
- Our team will design the right system based on your usage and backup needs
